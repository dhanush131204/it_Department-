About Our Department

The Information Technology Department at Kingston Engineering College is committed to providing high-quality education and research opportunities in the field of computer science. Our dedicated faculty members and cutting-edge curriculum ensure that students receive a well-rounded education in computer science.

We offer a wide range of courses, including multimedia and animation, web development, and more. Our experienced instructors are passionate about teaching and mentoring students to help them succeed in their academic and professional careers.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in computer science that prepares students for professional careers and advanced studies, while fostering innovation, research, and ethical values.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in computer science education and research, producing competent professionals who can contribute to technological advancements.
